Wong Kar Wai's World

Wong Kar Wai has been one of the most defining directors not only in Hong Kong but around the world. The renowned auteur is known for his distinct style of directing that includes kinetic camerawork, heavy use of music, and vivid cinematography that evoke the unique mood and atmosphere that each of his films have. Working with key collaborators like actors Maggie Cheung, Tony Leung, and Takeshi Kaneshiro, and cinematographer Christopher Doyle, Wong Kar Wai has created films about love, loss, loneliness, heartbreak, and happiness such as In the Mood for Love, Happy Together and Chungking Express that evoke such strong emotions in the hearts of their viewers that leave them feeling either melancholy or joyous.
