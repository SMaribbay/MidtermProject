Fallen Angels (1995)

Originally meant to be another segment of Chungking Express, Fallen Angels acts as the dark, volatile flip side of the former. Starring Leon Lai as the Hong-Kong-based hitman, who is about to complete his last hit but is distracted by his feelings for his elusive female partner (Michelle Reis). Takeshi Kaneshiro's character from the previous film returns to wreak havoc. Wong Kar Wai's signature trademarks remain present here with dizzy camerawork, use of music, and strong themes of loneliness and love.
