Happy Together (1997)

Wong Kar Wai creates one of his most emotionally raw and devastating films yet with Hong Kong high profile actors Tony Leung and Leslie Cheung starring as a couple travelling through Argentina trapped in an on-again, off-again relationship with each other hoping to resolve their issues. Wong Kar Wai seeks to depict the dynamics of a relationship with such intricacy and empathy and he does so with the help of his usual collaborator Christopher Doyle who uses vibrant, saturated colors to help create the tearjerking and heartaching film filled with both extreme happiness and sadness.
