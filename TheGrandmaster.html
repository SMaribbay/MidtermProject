The Grandmaster (2013)

Set against the backdroop of the Second Sino-Japanese War, Wong Kar Wai's most recent film to date sees the return of Tony Leung as the iconic Ip Man trying to provide for his family and Ziyi Zhang as the vindictive Gong Er seeking revenge for her father's death at the hands of Ma San. Wong Kar Wai explores unfamiliar territory directing a war/history/biography/romance film. Ambitiously implementing his usual trademarks in a martial arts film, Wong Kar Wai creates a unique visual and sensual experience that is filled with kinetic fight scenes and fascinating visuals that should keep the viewers' eyes glued to the screen.
